Publications

Art Dwells in the Present by Gregg Bordowitz

Published 2020

Featuring a preamble by Dean Marta Kuzma, Art Dwells in The Present marks the continuation of Yale School of Art Commencement—a series that publishes the speeches by speakers invited by the Dean on the occasion of the commencement of students receiving their MFA degrees from graphic design, painting/printmaking, photography, and sculpture.

Our guest speaker in May 2020 was Gregg Bordowitz. Fragmental editing by Angie Keefer, BA ‘99, and Willis Kingery, Graphic Design '19. Designed by Jinu Hong, Graphic Design '20, and Tuan Quoc Pham, Graphic Design '20.

30 pages, printed by Graphic Arts Studio, Chicago

Body of Work: Yale School of Art MFA 2019

Featuring the work of the 2019 MFA graduates

Published 2020

Featuring a foreword by Dean Marta Kuzma, and written contributions from Emma Gregoline, Graphic Design ‘19; Emile Mausner, Painting/Printmaking '19; Genesis Báez, Photography '19; and Alfredo Diaz, Sculpture '19; Body of Work showcases the collected work of the graduating MFA class of 2019, and marks the third installment of Yale School of Art’s all-school MFA book series.

Edited by Willis Kingery, Graphic Design '19; Edi Dai, Painting/Printmaking '19; and David Knowles, Graphic Design '19.

Designed by David Knowles, Graphic Design '19; and Willis Kingery, Graphic Design '19.

256 pages, printed by die Keure, Bruges

Yale School of Art MFA 2018

Featuring the work of the 2018 MFA graduates

Published 2019

Featuring a foreword by Dean Marta Kuzma, and edited by Ayham Ghraowi, Assistant Dean for Research and Public Projects, this publication showcases the collected work of the graduating MFA class of 2018, and marks the second installment of Yale School of Art’s all-school MFA book series.

Designed by Hrefna Siguroardottir, Graphic Design ‘18; Cover image: Dan Swindel, Photography '18, Untitled (2019).

253 pages, printed by Cassochrome, Belgium

Sermon of the Nonresponse by Fred Moten

Published 2018

Featuring a preamble by Dean Marta Kuzma, and published by the Yale School of Art Press, led in 2018 by Ayham Ghraowi, Assistant Dean for Research Public Projects Director, Yale School of Art Press. Designed by Bryce Wilner. Typeset in Linux Libertine.

34 pages, printed and bound in the United States by Regal Press
